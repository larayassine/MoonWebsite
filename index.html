a Dark Side

The Moon is tidally locked, meaning its rotation period matches its orbit around Earth. This results in one hemisphere always facing us while the other, known as the far side or "dark side" (though it receives sunlight too), is never visible from Earth.

idal Influence

TThe Moon's gravitational force pulls on Earth, primarily affecting the oceans, creating high and low tides. As Earth rotates, different areas of the planet experience this gravitational pull, leading to rising and falling sea levels.
